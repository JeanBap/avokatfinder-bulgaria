Имотен Регистър (Imoiten Registar)

The Имотен Регистър (Property Register) is Bulgaria's official register of real property rights — maintained by the Registry Agency and recording all ownership transfers, mortgages, and encumbrances.

The Имотен Регистър is administered by the Агенция по Вписванията (Registry Agency) under the Ministry of Justice. Bulgaria operates a deed-registration system rather than a title-registration system, meaning the register records documents (notarial deeds, mortgage contracts) rather than guaranteed titles.

Registration is effected by the notary submitting the notarialen akt to the Registry Agency immediately after signing. The registration fee is 0.1% of the declared property value. A вписване (entry) is made in the register, and the registered deed is returned with a registration stamp.

Because Bulgaria operates a deed rather than title system, due diligence requires checking the chain of ownership through all registered deeds — not just the current entry. A property may have had multiple transfers, and any defect in an earlier deed can affect current title.

The Registry Agency portal (portal.registryagency.bg) allows online searches of the Имотен Регистър by property identifier or owner name. Searches are subject to a small fee. Buyers should always pull a full history extract before signing.

Common Mistake: Relying solely on the current owner's notarialen akt without checking the full chain of deeds. Bulgaria's deed registration system means that a defect in any earlier transfer in the chain can invalidate the current owner's title.
Expert Tip: Instruct your Bulgarian lawyer to pull a full history extract from the Имотен Регистър covering all registered deeds on the property going back at least 10 years. This reveals any gaps, contested transfers, or encumbrances in the chain.

Encumbrances — hipoteki (mortgages), възбрани (attachments), and other rights — are recorded in the register against the property. A clean register extract confirms there are no registered encumbrances, though unregistered rights may still exist in limited circumstances.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of registration system does Bulgaria use?

Bulgaria uses a deed registration system rather than a guaranteed title system. The Имотен Регистър records documents (notarial deeds, mortgages) without guaranteeing the accuracy of earlier transactions. Due diligence must examine the full chain of deeds.

What is a вписване in the Bulgarian Property Register?

A вписване is a registration entry in the Имотен Регистър recording a deed, mortgage, or other encumbrance. The notary submits the document to the Registry Agency immediately after signing, and the вписване is made the same day.

How do I search the Bulgarian Property Register online?

Searches can be conducted via the Registry Agency portal at portal.registryagency.bg, searching by owner name or property identifier. A small fee applies. Full extract requests can be submitted online or in person at a Registry Agency office.
